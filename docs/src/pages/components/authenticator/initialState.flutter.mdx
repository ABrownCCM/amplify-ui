### Initial Step

By default, unauthenticated users are redirected to the Sign In step.

You can explicitly redirect to Sign Up or Reset Password as your initial step:

<Tabs>
  <TabItem title="Sign In">
    <Fragment>
      {({ platform }) => import(`./initialState.signIn.${platform}.mdx`)}
    </Fragment>
    <FlutterAuthenticatorExample
      initialStep={'signIn'}
    ></FlutterAuthenticatorExample>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem title="Sign Up">
    <Fragment>
      {({ platform }) => import(`./initialState.signUp.${platform}.mdx`)}
    </Fragment>
    <FlutterAuthenticatorExample
      initialStep={'signUp'}
    ></FlutterAuthenticatorExample>
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem title="Reset Password">
    <Fragment>
      {({ platform }) => import(`./initialState.resetPassword.${platform}.mdx`)}
    </Fragment>
    <FlutterAuthenticatorExample
      initialStep={'resetPassword'}
    ></FlutterAuthenticatorExample>
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>
